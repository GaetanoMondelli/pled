Digital Collaboration Framework


Once the rules of partecipation are set there is no tool to ensure their application

without going through long and sometimes expensive processes.

While smart contracts and blockchains can automatically make the rule enforcement

they require a deep technology knowledge, works only on certain scenarios 

and does not scale well and are diffult to update

 Without enforcing the rules by design, the general approach of DLTs can be a better trade off

as they offer flexibilityand allow to quickly take a fair decisions whether during conflict resolutions

or ensuring people are aware of and receiving the deserved benefits by make one view compatible with any another
